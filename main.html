about

Dear Master Jedi,

My knack for efficient parent communication is the envy of my school. Yet, it still takes me 15 minutes to complete and document contact with Johnny’s mom. I know that keeping up with parents will increase test scores 40% faster, but with such a time-consuming process, reaching all 87 of my students’ families is nearly impossible.

Sincerely,

Sleepless Do-It-All Teacher

Dear Sleepless Do-It-All Teacher,

I was there once too. Luckily, this is what the last Sleepless made for you:

Dash4Teachers is an iPhone app that allows teachers call more parents, more often. With Dash, teachers can log student performance and generate a daily speed dial of parent numbers. At the end of each conversation, Dash instantly creates a call log and analytics that inform future parent contact and ensure no student is overlooked. Parent engagement and data that teachers need to fast-track student success are now only a click away. Look for Dash in the iTunes store today.

Sincerely,

Master Jedi

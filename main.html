about

dash |daSH|

noun

1. a data-conscious Parent Relationship Management system.

2. a smartphone application that serves as a to-do list linked with parent phone numbers. This means no more time or data lost by making calls on one device and tracking on another.

Why Dash?

Three reasons to get started today!

1. Proven to increase student achievement. Parent teacher communication is proven to increase student GPA by 2 whole points, your students could improve from a 2.0 to a 4.0.

2. Effective and enjoyable. Parent teacher communication means better teacher student relationship.

3. Maximize the use of your lesson planning time to drive higher student achievement, instead of tracking disconnected phone numbers and calls.
